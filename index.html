Welcome to UIUCAlkemi.

UIUCAlkemi is a collaboration between Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory and the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. The goal of the collaboration is to produce a set of web-based tools for visualizing Machine Learning (ML) structures such as decision trees.

Status

We are actively developing the toolset and will announce when an alpha release is available
